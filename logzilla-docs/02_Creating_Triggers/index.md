At the heart of LogZilla's responsive capabilities lie the 'triggers'. These are predefined criteria that, when met, initiate a specific response. Think of a trigger as a watchful guardian, continuously monitoring event streams. Upon detecting a specified event, the trigger 'fires', setting in motion a defined reaction. This could range from recording the event's occurrence to initiating automated solutions.

In this section, we'll guide you through the process of creating and fine-tuning triggers, ensuring that you can optimize LogZilla's capabilities to suit your unique needs. Starting from the dashboard, where all your monitoring begins, to the detailed steps of defining a trigger, every facet will be covered.
